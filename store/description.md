Alfred streamlines your Shopify workflow. Built by a developer who got tired of clicking through endless menus and hunting for URLs.

Key Features

1. Appstore Partner Page
- Transform Shopify appstore's partner page into a clean, scannable table that actually makes sense.
- Understand all the apps at a glance
- Makes it super easy to find the info you need without endless navigation

2. Smart Shortcuts for Faster Navigation
Skip the clicking marathon with instant shortcuts:
- **Copy Theme Preview URL** - Grab the current theme's preview URL with all URL parameters included
- **Open in Admin** - Jump straight to admin pages for products, collections, pages, and articles
- **Open in Customizer** - Launch the theme customizer with your current page pre-loaded
- **Copy Product JSON** - Extract complete product data for competitor research, development and debugging
- **Copy Cart JSON** - Get full cart data instantly for testing and troubleshooting
- **Clear Cart** - Empty the cart with a single click

3. Collaborator Permission Presets (NEW!)
Save your precious time when requesting stores access:
- Save your frequently used permission combinations as reusable presets
- Apply permission presets instantly when requesting new collaborator access
- Manage, edit, and organize your presets in a clean interface

Why Alfred?

I built Alfred because I needed it. Every day, I was losing time to Shopify's labyrinthine interface. If you're tired of the same friction, Alfred might be exactly what you need.
