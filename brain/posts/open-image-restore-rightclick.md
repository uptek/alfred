# LinkedIn Post: Open Image in Admin & Restore Right-Click

Recently added a few updates to Alfred for Shopify.

**Open Image in Admin**: Right-click any image on a Shopify storefront to search for it in the store's Admin Files. Useful when you need to find or replace an asset but aren't sure where it lives.

**Restore Right-Click**: Some websites disable right-click menus and text selection. Alfred now automatically re-enables them. Toggle it off in Settings if you prefer the original behavior.

**Uninstall Survey**: If you decide to uninstall, a short survey helps me understand what could be better. Your feedback shapes future updates.
